Step 1. Find Your Subject

What is your subject? Anything from Astronomy to Classical Guitar, you can search for your subject and set your own learning goals. And since Sagefy is open-content, if you can't find your goal, you can make your own content and share with the world!

Try the Beta

Step 2. Choose Your Unit

Sagefy doesn't force you into any sort of timeline or specific order. When you select a subject to learn, you get to choose what lesson to focus on next. We'll make recommendations based on your prior knowledge. So whether to focus on finger-spelling instead of vocabulary for your Sign Language subject right now, its up to you.

Try the Beta

Step 3. Learn

Sagefy will present you with many different activities – videos, multiple choice questions, and interactive examples. We'll automatically update what content to show you based on your progress.

We'll help you keep track of your progress. And we'll help you stay working towards your learning goal.

  • Falling behind on your schedule? We'll send reminders.
  • Struggling with the material? We'll suggest some background information and practice to get you up to speed.
  • Finding it too easy? We'll fast forward.
  • Need more resources? We'll make suggestions based on past experiences from other learners.
